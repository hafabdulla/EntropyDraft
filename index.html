Global Talent Solutions

Hire, pay, and manage frontier teams-without the HR headache.

Entropy helps companies hire top talent across emerging markets-without setting up local entities or taking on employer risk. As your Employer of Record, we handle payroll, compliance, and contracts while you retain full operational control. We ensure simplicity, fast onboarding and regional hiring support you won't find elsewhere.

Simplify Operations

Let your team focus on what they do best. We handle payroll, contracts, and HR compliance across markets-so you spend less time on admin and more on execution.


Take back your time-we'll handle the paperwork.

Reduce Liability

We act as the legal employer, absorbing compliance and labor law exposure in each market. You maintain control of the team without taking on local liabilities.


We hire on paper, you lead in practice.

Hire Smarter, Scale Faster

We help you hire competitively across emerging markets using USD salaries. With deep local networks and compliant infrastructure, you gain the speed, insight, and reach to grow efficiently.
